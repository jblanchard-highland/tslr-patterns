The New School Lending Community (formerly known as cuLearn Connect) is a group of students, parents, and learners alike that share one question in common: How am I going to pay for college!? The student debt crisis has gotten out of hand. Together with Thrivent, we can make a difference in improving the crisis through education and resources!

By joining, Thrivent will connect you with:

  • Scholarship opportunities

  • Educational resources about how to pay for college

  • Learnings on how to be wise with money during the college years

You're also eligible to enter for a chance to win a $5,000 scholarship if you:

  • Are 18+

  • Are currently seeking an undergraduate or graduate degree

  • Do not reside in NY, FL or RI

See Official Rules for complete details on eligibility.
